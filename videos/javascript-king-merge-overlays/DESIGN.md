# JavaScript King Merge Overlays

Transparent 4K motion graphics for the merged-upload workflow.

- Canvas: 3840x2160 at 30 fps.
- Palette: near-black navy `#050B18`, navy `#081426`, electric blue `#1677FF`, cyan `#28E7FF`, luminous gold `#FFD21C`, cool white `#F7FAFF`.
- Typography: Impact, uppercase, condensed, with restrained electric-blue keyline.
- Motion: deterministic GSAP timelines, finite glows, fast signal traces, clean readable holds.
- Transition: 0.95 seconds, complete opaque coverage at the edit point.
- Lower-third: 3.2 seconds, dynamic title variable, gold ignition, cyan trace, navy unfold, scan reveal, one light sweep, fast collapse.
- Backgrounds remain transparent for WebM alpha rendering.
